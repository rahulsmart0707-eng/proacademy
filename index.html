The Challenge

Financial Barriers Limit Potential

Thousands of talented government college students aspire for SSC jobs, but lack access to quality guidance.

High Costs

Quality offline coaching often costs ₹10,000 to ₹20,000+, making it inaccessible for many government college students.

Location Barriers

Top coaching centers are concentrated in major cities, forcing students to relocate and incur heavy living expenses.

Unstructured Prep

Without proper mentorship, students rely on scattered free resources, leading to inefficient preparation and low selection rates.
